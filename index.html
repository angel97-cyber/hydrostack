How to play

  1. You get six items each day.
  2. Drag them into the correct order, top = most/highest/first.
  3. Hit Lock in. You get one shot per day.
  4. Each slot scores: 🟩 exact · 🟨 off by 1 · 🟥 off by 2+
  5. Share your grid. Come back tomorrow.

About Stackle

Stackle is a tiny daily ranking puzzle. New puzzle every midnight, your local time. No accounts, no logins, no tracking — just one quick stack a day.

If you enjoy it, you can drop a tip or unlock the full puzzle archive.

Built solo. Find us at usehydrostack.com.
